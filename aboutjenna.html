About Jenna Caldwell

The Creator.

Jenna Caldwell’s love for hair started quite young. Originally from Pennsylvania, Caldwell’s family has owned salons and beauty schools dating back to 1923. A natural to her craft, she started styling hair at 15 and never looked back.

Her passion sent her to California to attend cosmetology school then an externship at Carlton Hair in Orange County. Her Austin career began at Ruiz Salon, where she spent a year studying Aveda Hair color before stepping behind the chair.

As an Aveda color educator, her core values are inspire, educate, and grow. Her mission to live everyday intentionally shines through her body of work, and client relationships. Caldwell’s editorial work has most recently been recognized in The Colorist Magazine, and UK Hairdressers. She has styled for Austin Fashion Week, Austin Bridal Couture week, Kendra Scott, W for Worth, Julian Gold and many, many more.
